Seattle is in desparate need of a NHL team. Massive renovations are underway at the historic Key Arena. With the arena's update, it will now be able to host both NBA and NHL games. With the Seattle supersonics recent relocation to Oklahoma City, the wounds are still fresh for many basketball fans in Seattle. However, to only strive for returning the Supersonics might alienate another revenue maker: Professional Hockey. The average operating income for a NHL team is $18 million dollar

History

Seattle Metroolitans The Seattle Metropolitans were the First American team to win the Stanley cup.
Seattle has had a professional Hockey Team since 1924 when the Pacific Coast Hockey League folded.

Market

According to Nate Silver from the polling company, FiveThirtyEight.com, Seattle has the largest population of hockey supporters without fielding a Professional Hockey team.

NHL

As of the 2018 NHL hockey season, there are 24 American teams and 7 Canadian teams. In 2017 the NHL expanded to it's newest market, Las Vegas, Nevada with tremendous success. The Las Vegas Golden Knights are currently leading the league in success.
